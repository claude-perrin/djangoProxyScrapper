Ins'n'Outs

A personal budgeting app which saves your money.

It's simple. As you buy a cup of coffee or receive a salary, just enter that in application. It will provide youy not only with detailed monthly reports based on accounts you own and categories of your expenses/incomes, but also store all of your transactions in a hitory tab.

Read More

What have I learned?

  • Room Database - All the CRUD operations
  • Fragments and Navigation
  • Recycler Layout - populated with the data from the database
  • Generating files
  • Comparing to my previous app (Weather) - much cleaner architecture. I.e. all files
    are splitted between Activities, Adapters, Dialogs, Fragments, Room, and Util packages to
    make it understandable for other developers.

Built with

  • Kotlin - Programming language used
  • Dexter - For simpler permission handling
  • itextpdf - For generating PDF files
